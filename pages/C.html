Command & control

The Historical origin of the command and control meaning in technology came from the wars on battlefields when the right decision maker was the one who could see the big picture from all angles, leave nothing in the shadows, and communicate in real-time with his leaders to efficiently, fully utilize and manage all his resources for the right action. This enables a safe risk analysis which is a major factor in taking the element of surprise under control. Nile Icons help you to build this environment to give you the best practices for your industry to take you to the next level of command and control through these solutions

Group of functionally related rooms, co-located with the control room and including it, which houses the supporting functions to the control room, such as related offices, equipment rooms, rest areas, and training rooms

Crisis Room

You can find all the information you need in a crisis room to overcome a crisis. Senior management gathers here to assess the situation and make operational decisions while utilizing communication and technological resources. All internal and external hubs must be connected, and the systems must always be accessible. Nile Icons offers solutions to make sure your procedures run smoothly

Meeting Room

A distinctive collaborative meeting setting that combines in-person and remote participants in one session. With cutting-edge features like dedicated cameras for authentic eye contact and high-quality directional audio that guarantees a one-to-one connection, the interactive solution fosters real-time engagement. Breakout groups and highdefinition interactive whiteboards are among the integrated collaboration tools in the meeting room that enable interactive meetings for both in-person and remote participants besides using the meeting room reservation system to coordinate the process

Executive Room

By providing a secure, private setting for hybrid or virtual meetings that bring together in-person and remote participants, assists CEOs and their teams in interacting and developing strategies

WAR Room

The war room serves as a hub for ideas, a great place to brainstorm and generate fresh concepts, and a place to ensure that the strategy is flawless. Executives and project teams will spend the majority of their time setting up for the upcoming project in the war room. Every piece of information should be conveyed clearly. In every war room, there should be enough space to write, such as an interactive whiteboard. It should be the focal point of the room, somewhere where everyone can see and hear what is being said clearly.

Media command centre

An efficient social media centre not only identifies, displays, and analyses the dialogue surrounding your business, but also makes it possible for your social media staff to communicate with the rest of the organization effectively about the state of your brand. You can interact with your departments like customer service, marketing, and public relations more readily and respond to clients more quickly if you have a social media centre. Nile Icons can guide you through the process, from attractive command centre furniture and decoration to your data display and presentation
